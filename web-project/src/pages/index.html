About SAVI Software Solutions

Founded in 2023, we deliver excellence in software development and IT services.

SAVI Software Solutions was founded with the core mission of providing High-Quality design, Human Resources Management System, Payroll Processing System, School and Campus Management, Inventory Management System, e-Commerce, ERP, Web portal design & Hosting, Asset Management, Custom Application Development, Technology Consulting and IT infrastructure services.

We take on projects both big and small, work and really enjoy hammering out problems and getting results. We love taking on challenging projects that require full-on content strategy, thoughtful design, demanding development, and ongoing marketing.

Headquartered and fully equipped development center in Salem, we are a strong team with professionals experts, who work with integrity and passion to deliver world class IT solutions.
